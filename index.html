chicken

/ˈt͡ʃɪkɪn/

noun

Meaning

  • A domestic fowl, Gallus gallus, especially when young.

    Why do you refuse to fight? Huh, I guess you're just too chicken.

  • The meat from this bird eaten as food.
  • The young of any bird; a chick

Synonyms: biddy
